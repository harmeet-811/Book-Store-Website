How do I check the status of my order?

To check the status of your order, start by looking for a confirmation email that includes tracking information. This email will typically provide a link to track your order. Alternatively, log into your account on the store’s website or app, go to the “Order History” or “My Orders” section, and find your order to view its status. If you received a tracking number, you can enter it on the carrier’s website (e.g., UPS, FedEx, USPS) to get real-time updates. If you’re having trouble, contact customer support with your order number for assistance.

Can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order if it hasn’t been processed or shipped yet. Most stores allow cancellations within a certain window of time after placing the order. Check your order status in your account or contact customer support as soon as possible to request a cancellation. If the order has already been shipped, cancellation might not be possible, but you may still be able to return the item once it’s delivered.

What are the shipping charges?

Shipping charges are the fees added to the cost of your order to cover the delivery of your items. These charges can vary depending on factors like the shipping method (standard, express, etc.), the weight and size of your package, your location, and any promotions or discounts the store might offer. Some stores offer free shipping on orders over a certain amount, while others charge a flat rate or calculate fees based on distance or urgency. You can usually see the shipping charges at checkout before you complete your order.
